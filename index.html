ESG risk management
The process of identifying ESG risks associated with a business's operations and investments, acknowledging the negative consequences they can have on financial performance and reputation, and implementing actions to mitigate these risks
High-impact solutions
Initiatives, actions, or strategies that seek to address ESG-related challenges and meaningfully contribute to positive societal change (e.g., supporting microfinance institutions and initiatives that provide financial services to underserved populations)
The benefits that a business or investment can derive from incorporating ESG factors into its operations and investment strategy. This involves recognising the potential for sustainable practices to improve financial performance and industry reputation
ESG opportunities
The financial returns on an investment, measured against similar investments
